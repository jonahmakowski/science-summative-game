# Trivia Game
## Game Rules
- First person to press their respective key gets to take a hit at a question
- If the player gets it correct, they gain a certain amount of money, and are permitted to leave if they wish, and keep all their money
- If the player gets it incorrect, they are kicked out and lose all their money
- If there are two players left and one *chooses* to leave, the remaining player loses all their money and is kicked out
- If the player doesn't get the correct answer within the time limit they lose the same amount of money they would've gained if they got it correct
- If there are two players left and one is *kicked out*, the remaining player keeps all their money and leaves
- If nobody hits the buzzer with the time limit every player loses half the value of the question
- The goal is to leave with the maximum amount of money

## Values
- Question value: Every question the value is increased by 500 and starts at 1000
- Buzzer time: 20s
- Answering time: 5s
